There are many attractions in Guyana for visitors and residents alike. One major tourist attraction in Guyana that is very popular among residents and tourists is Lake Mainstay Resort which is located in Essequibo county within the Pomeroon-Supenaam region.

Lake Mainstay provides a wide variety of activities for people to enjoy such as Swimming, Biking, Cricket, Beach Volleyball and Fishing.

The lake also serves a very popular tourist attraction during major holidays such as New Year's Day, Republic Day, Easter Monday, Independence Day and Christmas Day since many people like to celebrate so that they can enjoy these special times with family and friends.

Another major tourist attraction in Guyana is Kaietur Falls which is the world's largest single drop waterfall located in the Potaro-Siparuni region and was discovered by Europeans during the 1870's.
